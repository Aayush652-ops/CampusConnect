# CampusConnect UX Overview

## Problem
Students miss out on valuable campus opportunities because information is scattered across WhatsApp groups, emails, posters, and multiple platforms.

## Solution
A centralized discovery platform that brings events, communities, internships, competitions, and opportunities together in one place.

## Key design goals
- Simplify event and opportunity discovery.
- Make information architecture easy to navigate.
- Surface personalized recommendations for students.
- Reduce friction by consolidating fragmented communication channels.

## Core screens
- Dashboard: personalized overview of relevant opportunities.
- Discovery: search and filter events, clubs, internships, and competitions.
- Community: browse student groups and campus communities.
- Saved: bookmark events and opportunities for later.

## Research and design process
- Conduct stakeholder interviews and student surveys.
- Define personas and map user journeys.
- Create low-fidelity wireframes and high-fidelity prototypes.
- Validate flows through usability testing.
